
Edge names should follow a pattern
   [zone]-V (Vert) -U (Up)               -1 (Variant)
                   -D (Down)
                   -M (Mid)
          H (Horz) -L (Left)
                   -R (Right)
                   -C (Center)
                   -UD (Up + Down)
                   -LR (Left + Right)   
                   -X (Other)


Mountain -> Mountain
[mont]-HL
[mont]-HR

=== Void ===
There's only one type of void connection. It means that this edge doesn't connect to another chunk. There may be another chunk on the other side, but they cannot connect.

[void]
